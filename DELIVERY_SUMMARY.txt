SCORING FORMULA
================================================================================

AI READINESS SCORE (0-100) =

  User Input Component (60 points max)
  ├─ Pain point readiness: 0-25 points
  ├─ Technology maturity: 0-20 points
  ├─ Business fit: 0-15 points
  └─ Industry baseline: 0-15 points

  + Inference Component (40 points max, Phase 2)
  ├─ Website analysis: 0-25 points
  └─ Public research signals: 0-15 points

  = Final Score: 0-100

EXAMPLE:
  20 (high-volume) + 18 (cloud) + 12 (good budget) + 12 (above average)
  = 62/100 (MODERATE READINESS)

With website analysis (+10):
  = 72/100 (STRONG READINESS)
