Top 3 Business Priorities

1 Tenant Communication Response Time Crisis

Property managers spend 60% of time on routine inquiries, causing slow response times that lose deals. With 45 properties across 3 states, this represents massive inefficiency and revenue loss. At readiness score 30, you need immediate wins with minimal technical complexity.

Intercom Inbox + Basic AI Resolution Bot Automation
Approach: Centralized tenant communication hub with AI-powered instant responses for common inquiries (lease terms, maintenance requests, payment info). Routes complex issues to appropriate property managers with full context.
Why This Fits: Given your readiness score of 30 and zero detected technology stack, this plug-and-play solution requires no technical expertise. Intercom's proven in property management with 2-week setup time.
Tools:
50% reduction in response time within 30 days, 30% time savings for property managers Low Complexity
2 Lease Document Chaos and Inefficiency

Manual lease paperwork processing across 45 properties creates bottlenecks, errors, and missed renewals. Property managers waste hours on document hunting and data entry instead of relationship building and deal closing.

Zapier + DocuSign + Google Workspace Automation Automation
Approach: Automated lease workflow system that triggers document creation, routing, and follow-ups based on simple triggers (lease expiration dates, tenant requests). No coding required - uses visual workflow builder.
Why This Fits: At readiness score 30, this uses familiar tools (likely already using email/docs) with simple trigger-based automation. Zapier's drag-and-drop interface requires zero technical skills but delivers immediate productivity gains.
Tools:
40% faster lease processing, 90% reduction in missed renewals within 60 days Low Complexity
3 Property Data Scattered Across Systems

Property information, tenant data, and communication history exists in emails, spreadsheets, and individual managers' heads. This creates knowledge silos, inefficient handoffs, and missed opportunities for proactive management.

HubSpot CRM (Free) + Notion AI Workspace Integration
Approach: Centralized property and tenant database with AI-powered search and organization. Notion AI helps organize property information and generate reports, while HubSpot tracks all tenant interactions and lease statuses in one place.
Why This Fits: Both platforms are designed for non-technical users. HubSpot's free tier provides robust CRM functionality, while Notion AI ($10/user) adds intelligent organization without complexity. Perfect for your current readiness level.
Tools:
25% improvement in tenant retention through proactive management, 60% faster information retrieval Low Complexity
